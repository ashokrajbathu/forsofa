About us

Founded in june of 2014 based in india, weber is a platform to search and find people,based on their activities,interests and location around the world.

Weber gives you the power to search for people, in every day life. start with morning, you can search for jogging partner in your location, friends who are interested in watching movie with you, people who want to play cricket etc... we are here to make your life simple by connecting people whom you want to meet at that moment. now in 6 major cities in india and exploring the world...

Mission

Bringing the world together with a search.
